How to make fried pelmeni by default

If you get bored by simple boiled pelmeni you should defenetly try these ones! Fried pelmeni!

Description

  • Ingridients
  • Recipe
  • Tips and Tricks

Ingridients

  1. pelmeni (1 kg)
  2. oil
  3. spices

Recipe

All you need is to be ready for some action now!

  1. Prepare your frying pan. Just it would be at hot temp.
  2. After that add some oil so it could go all over the pan.
  3. When the time comes - throw there all of your pelmenis. Do not close the pan as they will look more like boiled one. And we don't want that, do we?
  4. As long as pelmeni don't have fried skin we can prepare our souce
  5. For that we need two ingridients:
    • catchup
    • garlic
  6. Just mix them all and we have our adorable souce.
  7. Now your pelmeni must be ready already, just put them in the plate, add souce and voala!.. It's prepared
